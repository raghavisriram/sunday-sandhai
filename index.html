About Sunday Sandhai
Sunday Sandhai 'Mai' is a celebration dedicated entirely to the art of print and handcraft. This is a curated space where artists, block printers, ink practitioners, letterpress artisans, textile creators, and all makers of hand-printed works come together to showcase their craft. While we welcome diverse creative brands, the heart of Sunday Sandhai beats with the work of those who create with ink, block, hand, and intention. If your art involves the intimate relationship between hand and material, between vision and the printed word—you belong here.
